Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-03

E-Alpha Construction (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or submit an enquiry or quote request.

This policy applies to customers and visitors in New Zealand and Australia.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request or enquiry through our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Address or location details related to your enquiry
  • Project or service-related information you choose to provide

We only collect information that is necessary to respond to your enquiry or provide our services.

2. Automatic Location Detection

To route enquiries to the correct regional team, we may temporarily detect your country (e.g. New Zealand or Australia) based on your IP address.

  • We do not store IP addresses
  • We do not track users
  • We do not use IP information for marketing or analytics
  • Location detection is used only at the time of form submission

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or quote request
  • Contact you regarding your project or services
  • Internally route enquiries to the appropriate regional team (NZ or AU)
  • Improve our customer communication and service quality

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

4. Email Communications

When you submit a form on our website:

  • Your enquiry may be delivered to our New Zealand or Australian business email, depending on your location
  • You may receive a confirmation email acknowledging receipt of your enquiry
  • Email delivery is handled via a secure third-party service (EmailJS) and is used solely for enquiry communication.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, or unauthorised access. Personal information is accessed only by authorised personnel and is used strictly for business communication purposes.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information, where applicable

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us:

Email:

  • sales@e-alpha.co.nz (New Zealand)
  • sales@e-alpha.com.au (Australia)

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
