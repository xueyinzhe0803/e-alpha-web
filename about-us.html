Corporate History

2012

Company Founded

Alpha Construction NZ Ltd was established in Auckland, New Zealand, starting with scaffolding construction and rental services.

2014

Business Transition

Transitioned into importing and selling scaffolding products, becoming a comprehensive provider of building materials and scaffolding systems.

2015

Market Expansion and Partnership

Expanded our market presence across New Zealand, establishing strong partnerships with local construction companies.Partnership with Access Scaffolding and Co-founded Scaff 2 U Limited

2016

Innovation and Sustainable Development
